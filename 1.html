Civilization

Civilization refers to a highly developed human culture and society. In other words, the material, technical, and social development that humanity has achieved. It means a mode of life developed and refined in relation to primitive life as it is.

The characteristics of civilization distinguished from non-civilizational culture are the City, the Writing System, the Social-Cultural Center (as a visual communication means using symbolic symbols including letters) , State (State), and Higher Specialization of Labor (Complex State Society). Originally, Civilization is also derived from Latin Civis 'Citizens' and Civitas 'Cities / Countries'.
